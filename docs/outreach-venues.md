# South Florida Soccer Venues — DOC Walk-Up Guide

**How to use this:** Walk in Saturday/Sunday with your phone, find the DOC tent (not random parents), open the OffPitchOS demo, and ask for 90 seconds. Prioritize the multi-club complexes — you'll hit five DOCs in a morning instead of one.

Field counts and game windows below are best-effort from public listings; double-check the SFUYSA schedule (`sfuysa.com/fields`) Friday night before each trip for which clubs are actually playing.

---

## Broward County

### Plantation Central Park / Pine Island Park
- **Address:** 1201 NW 70th Ave, Plantation, FL 33313 (Pine Island Park is the soccer half — 9151 NW 2nd St, Plantation, FL 33324)
- **County:** Broward
- **Fields:** ~9 fields total — 6 natural grass + 1 lighted synthetic 11v11 + two 7v7 synthetic fields, plus a goalie practice area. SFUYSA codes PI1–PI7.
- **Best window:** Saturday 8am–1pm is league play across all age groups U8–U17. Sunday 9am–noon for the older travel ages.
- **Notable hosts:** Plantation FC Rush home base; SFUYSA league weekends bring Weston FC, Davie United, Coral Gables, Sunrise Surf in for crossovers.
- **Why go:** Highest-DOC-density complex in West Broward — Plantation Rush + visiting club tents on every Saturday morning.

### Brian Piccolo Sports Park
- **Address:** 9501 Sheridan St, Cooper City, FL 33024
- **County:** Broward
- **Fields:** 5 turf small-sided fields (three 6v6, one 7v7, one 9v9) operated as Soccer 5; plus the larger park's grass fields and velodrome.
- **Best window:** Saturday 9am–6pm has the most turnover (small-sided is back-to-back games). Sunday morning is open play / academies.
- **Notable hosts:** Soccer 5 USA Broward, PSG Academy Florida training, Raptors Soccer Academy.
- **Why go:** Constant churn of younger-age coaches between sessions — easy to grab three different academy directors in 90 minutes.

### West Pines United / Pembroke Pines (Walter C. Young + Pines Recreation)
- **Address:** 901 NW 129 Ave, Pembroke Pines, FL 33026 (Walter C. Young) and 19351 Taft St for WPU HQ
- **County:** Broward
- **Fields:** 1 lighted football/soccer field at Walter C. Young; West Pines United runs a multi-field tournament footprint across Pembroke Pines parks.
- **Best window:** Saturday all day during league season; their West Pines Soccer Fest and Kickoff Classic are tournament weekends with 100+ teams.
- **Notable hosts:** West Pines United F.C., Pembroke Pines rec leagues, Pembroke Pines Optimist (Piranhas).
- **Why go:** WPU is one of the largest competitive travel programs in South Florida — DOC Todd Goodman is on-site most Saturdays.

### Vista View Park
- **Address:** 4001 SW 142nd Ave, Davie, FL 33330
- **County:** Broward
- **Fields:** ~6 multipurpose grass fields (former landfill, sloped — used heavily for rec and small-sided)
- **Best window:** Saturday 8am–noon for rec leagues; Sunday morning is travel team practice slots.
- **Notable hosts:** Davie United / Davie Sharks, Hollywood-area travel teams, GHSL recreational.
- **Why go:** GHSL hub — recreational coaches here are exactly the $149-tier conversion you want.

### Markham Park
- **Address:** 16001 W State Rd 84, Sunrise, FL 33326
- **County:** Broward
- **Fields:** Multipurpose sports fields inside a 669-acre park; not a primary tournament site but used for practices and small leagues.
- **Best window:** Saturday morning rec; Sunday afternoon overflow practices.
- **Notable hosts:** Sunrise Surf, Sunrise Sting youth programs.
- **Why go:** Catch Sunrise Surf and Sunrise rec coaches together — secondary venue, only worth it if you're already in West Broward.

### Tradewinds Park
- **Address:** 3600 W Sample Rd, Coconut Creek, FL 33073
- **County:** Broward
- **Fields:** Soccer fields inside a 625-acre park; Soccer 5 Coconut Creek operates small-sided turf adjacent at the park.
- **Best window:** Saturday 9am–1pm rec games; Sunday morning small-sided.
- **Notable hosts:** Coconut Creek Youth Soccer League, Margate United FC overflow, Soccer 5 Coconut Creek leagues.
- **Why go:** North Broward's main multi-club Saturday venue — Margate, Coconut Creek, Coral Springs coaches all converge.

### C.B. Smith Park
- **Address:** 900 N Flamingo Rd, Pembroke Pines, FL 33028
- **County:** Broward
- **Fields:** Multipurpose fields used by Pembroke Pines rec and overflow travel.
- **Best window:** Saturday morning recreational waves (5–12 yo).
- **Notable hosts:** Pembroke Pines youth programs, GHSL teams.
- **Why go:** Recreational-side coaches/team parents only — skip unless you've already hit West Pines.

### Florida Blue Training Center (Inter Miami CF Academy)
- **Address:** 1350 NW 55th St, Fort Lauderdale, FL 33309
- **County:** Broward
- **Fields:** 6 natural grass + 1 turf, 50,000 sq ft training building. Adjacent to Chase Stadium (19,100 cap).
- **Best window:** MLS NEXT match days for U13–U17 are Saturdays/Sundays — schedule on intermiamicf.com/academy.
- **Notable hosts:** Inter Miami CF Academy U12–U17 (top of SoFla pyramid).
- **Why go:** Not for cold walk-ups (gated, professional staff) — but on academy match days the **visiting** clubs pull up: Schulz, Strike Force, Boca United. Stand near the visiting team lot.

### Sunrise Athletic Complex
- **Address:** 14600 NW 8th St, Sunrise, FL 33325
- **County:** Broward
- **Fields:** Multipurpose grass fields, lighted.
- **Best window:** Saturday morning rec games.
- **Notable hosts:** Sunrise rec leagues, Sunrise Surf.
- **Why go:** Lightweight rec backup; only if Markham is already done.

## Palm Beach County

### Patch Reef Park
- **Address:** 2000 Yamato Rd, Boca Raton, FL 33431
- **County:** Palm Beach
- **Fields:** 3 multipurpose synthetic-turf fields (converted 2019); also 3 baseball fields at the same complex.
- **Best window:** Friday nights and Saturday mornings 8am–noon are the SABR youth game windows; Saturday afternoons travel.
- **Notable hosts:** SABR / Team Boca primary home; FAU community games.
- **Why go:** Highest density of competitive Palm Beach DOCs on a single morning — Team Boca + visiting Boca Juniors, Boca United on rotation.

### Spanish River Athletic Park (Countess de Hoernle Park)
- **Address:** 1000 Spanish River Blvd, Boca Raton, FL 33431
- **County:** Palm Beach
- **Fields:** 4 lighted soccer fields on the 80-acre Spanish River Athletic Complex (former IBM land).
- **Best window:** Saturday 8am–6pm; Sunday morning travel windows.
- **Notable hosts:** SABR, Team Boca, Boca Raton FC youth, FC Boca Predators.
- **Why go:** Backup to Patch Reef when Patch is full — same DOC pool, slightly more parent traffic.

### John Prince Park (Soccer 5 Lake Worth)
- **Address:** 2520 Lake Worth Rd, Lake Worth, FL 33461
- **County:** Palm Beach
- **Fields:** 2 5v5 fields + 4 7v7 small-sided fields (opened 2023); 7 days a week.
- **Best window:** Saturday 9am–5pm league play for U8–U13; Sunday morning academies.
- **Notable hosts:** Soccer 5 Palm Beach, Boynton United FC overflow, mid-county academies.
- **Why go:** Central PB County small-sided hub — easy to hit Boynton, Lake Worth, and Greenacres rec coaches in one stop.

### Palm Beach Gardens / PBG Predators (Mirasol Park, Lilac Park, Plant Drive Park)
- **Address:** Multiple — Mirasol Park 12385 Jog Rd, PBG, FL 33418; Plant Drive 4255 Plant Dr, PBG, FL 33410
- **County:** Palm Beach
- **Fields:** PBGYAA / PBG Predators run across multiple city parks with 8+ fields combined.
- **Best window:** Saturday all day (rec + travel); Friday nights for older age groups.
- **Notable hosts:** PBG Predators (ECNL boys), PBGYAA recreational, Palm Beach Soccer Academy.
- **Why go:** North-county anchor — Predators is ECNL, brings in serious DOCs; rec side is the affordable-tier conversion.

### Wellington Village Park / Wellington Soccer Club fields
- **Address:** 11700 Pierson Rd, Wellington, FL 33414
- **County:** Palm Beach
- **Fields:** Wellington SC home complex — 8+ fields used for league and Wave travel.
- **Best window:** Saturday 8am–4pm league play; Friday nights older age groups; Sundays travel/practice.
- **Notable hosts:** Wellington Soccer Club (Wave) — ECNL boys/girls, 1,000+ players.
- **Why go:** Largest single-club youth program in Palm Beach — DOC Patrick Zoete reachable by walk-up; visiting clubs tent up on tournament weekends.

### Sunset Cove / South County Regional Park
- **Address:** 12551 Glades Rd, Boca Raton, FL 33498 (south county regional)
- **County:** Palm Beach
- **Fields:** Multi-purpose fields adjacent to the amphitheater complex; used by SABR overflow and west Boca rec.
- **Best window:** Saturday morning rec; Sunday morning practice.
- **Notable hosts:** SABR programs, Greater Boca YSA / Boca United training overflow.
- **Why go:** Skip unless Patch + Spanish River are already covered — small-volume complement.

### AC Delray Rush / Delray Beach Soccer Club fields
- **Address:** 2505 N Seacrest Blvd, Delray Beach, FL 33444
- **County:** Palm Beach
- **Fields:** Delray Beach municipal fields, 4–6 used by AC Delray Rush.
- **Best window:** Saturday 8am–noon rec + travel; Sunday morning practice.
- **Notable hosts:** AC Delray Rush (DOC Stephan von Lattorff), Delray Beach SC rec.
- **Why go:** Mid-county targeted hit — DOC named, walk-up will likely actually find him on Saturday morning.

### Royal Palm Beach Commons Park
- **Address:** 11600 Poinciana Blvd, Royal Palm Beach, FL 33411
- **County:** Palm Beach
- **Fields:** Multiple lighted multipurpose fields.
- **Best window:** Saturday 8am–2pm.
- **Notable hosts:** RPB Strikers (Royal Palm Beach Soccer), Wellington Wave overflow.
- **Why go:** RPB Strikers DOC is Mal Hasan; west-county rec coaches on hand.

### Jupiter Community Park
- **Address:** 3377 Church St, Jupiter, FL 33458
- **County:** Palm Beach
- **Fields:** 6+ multipurpose fields.
- **Best window:** Saturday morning, Sunday morning.
- **Notable hosts:** Jupiter United, Rise FC, Tequesta-area rec.
- **Why go:** Northern PB anchor — two competitive academies (Jupiter United DOC + Rise FC DOC Malcolm Boyle) plus rec side.

## Miami-Dade County

### Tropical Park
- **Address:** 7900 SW 40th St (Bird Rd), Miami, FL 33155
- **County:** Miami-Dade
- **Fields:** 6+ multipurpose fields; Soccer 5 Academy operates a turf footprint inside the park.
- **Best window:** Saturday 8am–6pm constant rotation; Sunday morning small-sided + tryouts.
- **Notable hosts:** Soccer 5 Academy, Strike Force FC tryouts, multiple SFUYSA clubs use Tropical for crossovers.
- **Why go:** Center-of-Miami density — 4–6 different clubs every Saturday morning.

### Kendall Soccer Park
- **Address:** 8011 SW 127th Ave, Miami, FL 33183
- **County:** Miami-Dade
- **Fields:** 4G turf, multiple small-sided fields under Soccer 5 Kendall + grass for KSC/Miami Rush Kendall.
- **Best window:** Saturday 9am–8pm; Sunday morning tryouts/leagues.
- **Notable hosts:** Miami Rush Kendall SC (formerly Kendall Soccer Coalition), Soccer 5 Kendall.
- **Why go:** Miami Rush is one of the bigger Miami programs and uses this as their main HQ — DOC accessible.

### Continental Park
- **Address:** 10000 SW 82nd Ave, Miami, FL 33156
- **County:** Miami-Dade
- **Fields:** Reopening per soil remediation (east phase 2025); historically 4–5 multipurpose fields.
- **Best window:** Saturday morning when fields are open; check status before driving.
- **Notable hosts:** Pinecrest Premier, Palmetto Bay youth, Coral Gables travel.
- **Why go:** Pinecrest/Palmetto Bay families — affluent, willing to pay; verify field status before trip.

### Tamiami Park
- **Address:** 11201 SW 24th St (Coral Way), Miami, FL 33165
- **County:** Miami-Dade
- **Fields:** 247-acre park; multiple soccer fields used by Miami FC Academy as home.
- **Best window:** Saturday all day Miami FC Academy + visitors; Sunday morning rec.
- **Notable hosts:** Miami FC Academy (USL Academy), youth recreational leagues.
- **Why go:** Miami FC Academy is the second pro-affiliated pyramid in Miami — visiting clubs roll through on game days.

### Miami-Dade County Youth Fair Park / Country Club of Miami fields (NW Miami-Dade)
- **Address:** Country Club of Miami area, 6801 Miami Gardens Dr, Hialeah, FL 33015
- **County:** Miami-Dade
- **Fields:** Multi-field complex used by NW Miami-Dade clubs.
- **Best window:** Saturday morning rec + travel.
- **Notable hosts:** Hialeah City FC, North Miami / Miami Gardens rec.
- **Why go:** Underserved NW Miami-Dade — affordable-tier clubs that haven't seen modern tooling.

### Miami Rush / Miami Futbol Academy (Olympia Heights / FIU area)
- **Address:** Training at facilities near FIU campus + Tamiami area
- **County:** Miami-Dade
- **Fields:** Multi-field training footprint.
- **Best window:** Saturday MLS Next match windows; Sunday training overflows.
- **Notable hosts:** Miami Futbol Academy Rush (MLS Next boys; DPL girls).
- **Why go:** MLS Next club — visiting club staff are present on home weekends.

### Cutler Bay / Bel Aire Park (Albion SC Miami home)
- **Address:** 18500 Franjo Rd, Cutler Bay, FL 33157 (Bel Aire) + 10100 SW 200th St (Albion SC HQ area)
- **County:** Miami-Dade
- **Fields:** Bel Aire 4–5 multipurpose fields used by Albion SC Miami.
- **Best window:** Saturday 8am–2pm; Sunday morning practice.
- **Notable hosts:** Albion SC Miami (formerly Cutler Ridge SC) — U8–U19 boys and girls.
- **Why go:** South Miami-Dade's main competitive home — Albion DOC is reachable in person.

### Goulds Park
- **Address:** 11350 SW 216th St, Miami, FL 33170
- **County:** Miami-Dade
- **Fields:** New synthetic turf field (post-$1.7M renovation); used by South Miami-Dade rec.
- **Best window:** Saturday morning rec.
- **Notable hosts:** Miami FC community programs, South Miami-Dade rec.
- **Why go:** Underserved area, single small-sided turf — only if you're already south for Albion.

### Doral Meadow Park / City of Doral fields
- **Address:** 11555 NW 58th St, Doral, FL 33178 + Doral Central Park
- **County:** Miami-Dade
- **Fields:** Multi-field complex used by Doral Soccer Club.
- **Best window:** Saturday all-day Doral SC league play.
- **Notable hosts:** Doral Soccer Club (recreational + competitive).
- **Why go:** Doral SC is the largest single-city club in Miami-Dade by player count — high-traffic Saturday.

### Devon Aire Park
- **Address:** 10400 SW 122nd Ave, Miami, FL 33186
- **County:** Miami-Dade
- **Fields:** Multipurpose fields, walking track, baseball.
- **Best window:** Saturday morning rec.
- **Notable hosts:** Local Kendall/West Kendall rec leagues.
- **Why go:** Small-volume — only worth a stop if you're already at Kendall Soccer Park.
